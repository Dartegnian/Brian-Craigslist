Vision

NASA. Space Exploration. Because of their aggressiveness towards the prospect of exploring the cosmos, these two words are synonymous to many an ear. Originally driven by military needs, it is now a major hub of development, specializing in Earth Science through space exploration, capitalizing in human resources as well. Armstrong said that they fly what others only imagine, and I, for one, does not see the need to be left out.


Mission

The Manila Mission Control project aims to aid in the better understanding of the various technologies NASA develops. Not only that, but we also aim for educating the minds of the youth so that they may be interested in exploring the world around them at an early age.


Accomplishments

It is no doubt that NASA has brought a garnered of accomplishments within the past few decades of its existence. Ever since its early beginnings, it revolutionized the way people move and go about the world by daring to look up. What set the agency apart from the others is that they had the necessary people to drive innovation toward the stars, and their thirst for exploration has proved to be very fruitful thus far.


NASA has since then been playing a major role in space innovations such as the construction of the Hubble Space Telescope, the International Space Station, the various satellite missions toward the other planets, and so on. Since then, they have started to take a more focused interest in helping out other countries achieve the goals they have achieved throughout their years.
